Account Deletion

To delete your account, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Access Your Profile

Navigate to your profile page within the application. This is usually accessible by clicking on your username or profile picture.

Step 2: Locate the Delete Option

Look for an option labeled "Delete Account," "Account Settings," or something similar within your profile section.

Step 3: Confirmation

Carefully read any warnings or information presented before confirming the deletion. Account deletion is typically irreversible.

Important Note: If you cannot find an option to delete your account within the application, please contact our support team for assistance.
